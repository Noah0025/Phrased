## Key Concepts
- RTM (Reactive Transport Modeling): couples fluid flow, solute transport, and geochemical reactions
- Darcy's Law: q = -K * (dh/dl) — governs groundwater flow
- ADE (Advection-Dispersion Equation): describes solute transport with advection, dispersion, reaction
- TST (Transition State Theory) rate law: controls mineral dissolution/precipitation rates
- REV (Representative Elementary Volume): scale at which continuum assumptions hold
- Upscaling: deriving macro-scale parameters from pore-scale processes
- Incomplete mixing: Cirpka's key research area — reactions slower when mixing is incomplete
